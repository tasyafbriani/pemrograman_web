VIU

Description

Viu is an over-the-top video service operated by PCCW Media, a subsidiary of PCCW. Debuting in Hong Kong, Viu provides Asian drama series, especially Korean dramas, anime variety programs and entertainment news.

As of December 2018, Viu had 30 million monthly active users.
